About Us

At DealMart Restaurant, we take pride in serving authentic Indian and Western cuisine. Our chefs use the freshest ingredients to create mouthwatering dishes that will leave you wanting more.

Whether you're looking for a cozy dinner for two or a large family gathering, we have the perfect setting for you. Join us for an unforgettable dining experience!

Our restaurant is designed to provide a warm and inviting atmosphere, making it the perfect place to enjoy a meal with family and friends. We also offer catering services for special events and occasions.

Menu

We offer a variety of delicious dishes to satisfy your cravings. Our menu includes a range of vegetarian and non-vegetarian options, as well as Western dishes.

Our menu is designed to cater to all tastes and preferences. Whether you're a fan of Indian cuisine or prefer Western dishes, we have something for everyone. Enjoy your meal!
